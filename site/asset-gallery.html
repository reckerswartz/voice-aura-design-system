Asset Guidelines

Quick reference for sourcing, optimizing, and attributing assets. Full details in ASSET_GUIDELINES.md.

5-Gate Acquisition Flow

GateCheckAction
1Existing assetsSearch assets/ first — avoid duplicates
2LicenseISC, MIT, CC0, SIL OFL = approved. Freepik/Flaticon free = conditional (attribution)
3Visual consistencyMust match 1.5px stroke, 24px grid, rounded caps
4OptimizationSVGO (svgo.config.js), max 3 KB per icon, 50 KB per illustration
5DocumentationAdd entry to ATTRIBUTIONS.md
